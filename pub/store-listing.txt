Full description:
This app adds Greater Sudbury Transit buses information to MonTransit.

This app provides the planned schedule as well as Real Time Transit Data from the City of Greater Sudbury MyBus API.

Greater Sudbury Transit buses serve Sudbury, Azilda, Blezard Valley, Capreol, Chelmsford, Coniston, Copper Cliff, Elmview, Falconbridge, Garson, Hanmer, Laurentian University, Lively, Minnow Lake, Val Caron, Val Thérèse in Ontario, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the City of Greater Sudbury.
http://opendata.greatersudbury.ca/

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-sudbury-transit-bus-android

This app is not related with Greater Sudbury Transit and the City of Greater Sudbury.

Permissions:
- Other: required for real time data from mybus.greatersudbury.ca.
